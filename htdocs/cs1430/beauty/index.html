When Choices are Limited

Which fruit is your favorite?
Are there any other adorable animals you love?
How about your aesthetic preference?
And your daily routines?

Did you notice something?

that..

those who prefer other fruits, adore other pets

admire other arts, wear no makeup

and more..

have they been considered?

It seems they don't have a choice here

most things do offer choices

However...

many people don't have a choice

when it comes to BEAUTY PRACTICES

Learn more about beauty practices

and what we can do

to create a world where everyone belongs.
